Dose Round Tab — Omitted States

States left out of the base Dose Round Tab screen: the closed/past dose round table, and every modal triggered from the tab. Each modal is shown statically on its backdrop; open/close, focus-trap and Esc are Angular behaviours (see file header). AIN-only modals are out of MVP scope and excluded.

A. Closed / past dose round

Reason and Admin controls disabled; each row gains Paper Signed and Incident Report Logged actions. Clicking either removes the medication and saves the record.

Past dose round — read only. Record via Paper Signed or Incident Report Logged.

B. Confirm administration — dosage mismatch

Medium modal. The warning strip appears only when Given ≠ Prescribed; without it, this is the standard confirmation.

Confirm Administration for SMITH, John

Warfarin 5mg

Prescribed 1 tab · Given 2 tabs · Oral · 1700

The dose being given (2 tabs) differs from the prescribed dose (1 tab). Confirm you intend to administer the amount entered.

C. Secondary Authentication (dual sign)

Small modal. Witnessing RN/EN enters their credentials to co-sign. Agency users cannot dual sign; an unauthorised PIN follows the same error path as elsewhere.

Secondary Authentication Required

A second authorised user must confirm administration of Actrapid Insulin.

D. BGL Reading Required

Small modal. Entering a value here opens the Insulin Sliding Scale Directions modal (E).

BGL Reading Required

Record the resident's BGL before administering Insulin.

E. Insulin Sliding Scale Directions (expanded)

Large "Form + warning" modal. Shown in its expanded state — the Reason For Change section appears once the administered dosage differs from the calculated value and must be completed before proceeding. In the collapsed state that section is hidden.
