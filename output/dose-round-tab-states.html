Dose Round Tab — Omitted States

Rebuilt on components.md v3. Confirmation/alert modals (B, C, D, H, I) follow the v3 confirmation pattern (400px, 16px radius, 48px featured icon, divider, 44px close). Table/form modals (E, F, G) are not a documented v3 type — they use a flagged panel shell built from shared v3 primitives and need separate extraction. Open/close, focus-trap and Esc are Angular behaviours.
